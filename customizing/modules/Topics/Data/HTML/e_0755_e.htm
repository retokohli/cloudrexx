Insurance on the life of a third party

With insurance on the life of a third party, the policyholder concludes a life insurance policy on the life of a third person, not on his/her own life. Thus the policyholder and the insured person are not one and the same person. Only the policyholder is entitled to the rights and obligations under the insurance contract, not the insured person. The conclusion of an insurance policy on the life of a third party requires prior written agreement from the insured third party in order to be valid (VVG/LCA 74).
