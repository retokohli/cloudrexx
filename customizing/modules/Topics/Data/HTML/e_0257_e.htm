Partial premium payments (paid-up component)

An insured capital sum may consist of one part where premiums are not paid and one part with periodic payment of premiums. For the former, the customer pays no premiums; the paid-up part was either set up with a single premium paid at the commencement of the insurance contract or during the term of the contract as a result of a change to the contract (e.g. reduction of the sum insured).
