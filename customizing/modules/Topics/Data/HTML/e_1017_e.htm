Excess of loss reinsurance

Excess of loss reinsurance is non-proportional reinsurance.
Excess of loss per risk reinsurance applies to a single insured risk (i.e. the insured object). For this insured risk, the reinsurer assumes the portion of a loss that exceeds its deductible up to the upper limit of cover.
Excess of loss per event reinsurance applies to an insured event. For this insured event, the reinsurer assumes the portion of a loss that exceeds its deductible up to the upper limit of cover.
