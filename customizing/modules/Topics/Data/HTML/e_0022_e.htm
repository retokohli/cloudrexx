Scope

The term 'scope' refers to the specified limits within which insurance is provided. For each individual insurance policy, these limits may relate to:
- the minimum and maximum insured sum,
- the minimum and maximum age at entry,
- the minimum and maximum age at term,
- the minimum and maximum duration of a contract
etc.

Other provisions are also included in the scope, such as:
- the maximum amount of additional cover supplementary to the main insurance,
- the option of including one or more additional persons,
- whether the desired tariff is permitted as a linked savings plan policy
etc.
