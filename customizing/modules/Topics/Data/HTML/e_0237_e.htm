Debenture, bond

Security which represents a monetary provision and is generally used to raise capital in the medium or long term. The rate of interest on this kind of security remains constant throughout its period of validity (though there are exceptions to this) and is based on the level of current interest rates on the money markets, the duration of validity and the debtor's solvency. The security itself is known as a debenture or bond and represents one unit of total capital borrowings.
In current parlance, the word bond also implies a financial commitment between two or more persons where one party represents the debtor and the other the creditor.
