Delta of an option

The delta of an option is a measure of sensitivity which indicates the extent to which an option participates in the movement of the underlying instrument (e.g. the SMI). If an option on a Swiss equity has a delta of 0.4 (or 40%), it gains CHF 0.40 for each Swiss franc that the share price rises and loses CHF 0.40 for each Swiss franc that the share price falls.
