Waiting time

A waiting time is a period of time between the occurrence of an insured event and the date on which the insurance company is contracted to commence indemnity payments. A waiting time is applied in the area of disability pensions and where daily indemnities are paid out by a life insurance company. Waiting times offer the possibility of coordinating the payment of benefits where other insurance institutions are covering the same risk. A waiting time also allows the obligation incumbent on an employer to continue to pay a salary to be taken into account. Waiting time must not be confused with period of non-availability.
