Insurance of buildings

Insurance of buildings covers damages caused to the building and to construction materials by fire, water and glass breakage. Fixed interior fittings such as wall cupboards, ovens, cookers, baths, window blinds, and so on are also covered by this insurance. The insurance of buildings for damages caused by fire is mandatory in most cantons.
The cover and premiums vary from canton to canton. The insurance normally covers damage caused by fire, lightning and explosions as well as natural damage.
