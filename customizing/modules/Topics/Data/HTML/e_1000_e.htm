FINMA

The Financial Market Supervisory Act FINMAG combined the three regulatory authorities the Federal Office of Private Insurance (FOPI), the Federal Banking Commission (FBC) and the Swiss Anti-Money Laundering Control Authority (AML CA) under the single Swiss Financial Market Supervisory Authority FINMA. The purpose of the Financial Market Supervisory Authority is to protect creditors, investors, insured persons and the overall functioning of the financial markets.
