Housebreaking (burglary)

Housebreaking (burglary) is a type of theft. A housebreaker (burglar) forces entry into a building or the interior of a building or breaks into a receptacle once inside. Also considered to be housebreaking (burglary) is a theft committed after gaining entry to a building or after opening a receptacle using a key or code obtained by housebreaking (burglary) or by robbery.
