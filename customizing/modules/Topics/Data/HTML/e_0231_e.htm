Guarantee of extended cover

Some insurers offer their customers a guarantee of extended cover without charging an additional premium (also known as a guarantee of insurability). At specific time intervals, or in the event of occurrences specified in the GCI such as marriage, birth of a child, or becoming self-employed, the policyholder may increase the insurance benefits without having to undergo a medical examination. Restrictions may be imposed in case the insured event occurs shortly after the benefits are increased. If the in-sured person is unable to work, no benefits will be provided for the increased portion.
