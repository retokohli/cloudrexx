Damages

Damages are monetary losses amounting to the difference between the injured party's actual fortune and his fortune as it would have been had the event causing the damages not taken place. In principle all damages or loss is taken into account as a result of the event, including any lost income which would have resulted in an increase in fortune. A distinction is made between corporal, material and economic damages.
Note: the Road Traffic Act does not make the distinction between corporal damages and material damages. In the RTA economic damages suffered as a result of corporal or material damages are considered as corporal or material damages and are indemnified as such.
