All-in fee

An all-in fee is charged to the funds assets and covers the costs incurred in connection with the funds management and administration of the fund assets. This includes expenses incurred for the purchase, sale and safekeeping of securities, plus all other related expenses (printing prospectuses, annual and semi-annual reports, costs for auditing and publication of prices, fees charged by the supervisory authority, etc.). Which costs are included in the All-in Fee is different from one management company to another. The details are included in the respective fund regulations.
