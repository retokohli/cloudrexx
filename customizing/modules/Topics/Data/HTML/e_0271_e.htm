Regional banks

Regional banks and savings banks are among the oldest Swiss banks. Their objective is to provide secure and profitable savings investments. Even today the term savings bank implies a financial institution whose third-party funds are entirely savings funds. Nowadays most of the invested funds are used to provide mortgages whereas in the past regional bank activities included almost all banking operations, both interest and non-interest business, on a much broader scale. The banks are very well established in their regions and have as traditional customers business, industry, agriculture and private individuals.
