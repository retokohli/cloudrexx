Duty to due care and attention

The Swiss Civil Code is based on the principle that each individual can expect other persons to display due care and attention. Hence, all policyholders are expected to show such care and attention. A person who willingly ignores the rules of damage sprevention exposes himself to a reduction in insured benefits. If a person causes damages through not having applied due care and attention, he is said to show a lack of duty to due care and attention.
